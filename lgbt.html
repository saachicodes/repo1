The LGBTQ+ Community

Those of sexual orientations or gender identities other than heterosexuality and cisgender in today’s society suffer greatly from other people’s ignorance, discriminative behaviour and laws globally that discriminate against them.Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual (as well as pansexual, asexual+) people and Transgender people are often unrecognised or forgotten in society, OR are harassed and abused just for whom they love.

Representation in the Media

Firstly, LGBTQ+ characters are not widely represented because of our heteronormative and cisnormative society (meaning a society that treats being heterosexual as the default and normal, and therefore alienates other sexualities). 82 out of the 100 Top films of 2014 fail to include an LGBTQ+ character. And when they are, they are often based on harmful stereotypes and have no depth or strength as a character.
