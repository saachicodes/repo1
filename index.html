LGBT+

Those of sexual orientations or gender identities other than heterosexuality and cisgender in today’s society suffer greatly from other people’s ignorance, discriminative behaviour and laws globally that discriminate against them. Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual (as well as pansexual, asexual+) people and Transgender people are often unrecognised or forgotten in society, OR are harassed and abused just for whom they love.
